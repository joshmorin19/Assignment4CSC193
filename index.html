Assignment 2: Apex Legends Character

Loba is an playable character in Apex Legends. When playing as Loba you gain access to support legend abilities, alongside her personal abilities. These legend specific abilites: A Burglar's Best Friend, this allows her to throw a bracelet to teleport to a different location; Eye for Quality, this allows her to see rare loot through walls; Black Market Boutique, this allows her to place her black market and loot items within a small radius of the market.
